Button Group

Group buttons together for toolbars and related actions.

The <luna-button-group> component groups buttons together for toolbars and related actions.

Paths

/lunadom/components/button-group/button-group.js REQUIRED
/lunadom/components/button/button.js REQUIRED
/lunadom/components/dropdown/dropdown.js
/lunadom/components/menu/menu.js

Overrides

@slot default - Buttons to group
@attr pill - Whether the group has pill shape
@attr spacing - Space between buttons
@attr vertical - Whether the group is vertical
@attr fullwidth - Whether the group takes full width
--luna-button-group-spacing - Button group spacing
