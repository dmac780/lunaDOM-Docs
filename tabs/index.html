Tabs

Tab navigation with closable tabs and multiple placements.

The <luna-tab-group>, <luna-tab>, and <luna-tab-panel> components provide tab navigation with multiple placements and closable tabs.

Paths

/lunadom/components/tab/tab.js REQUIRED
/lunadom/components/tab-group/tab-group.js REQUIRED
/lunadom/components/tab-panel/tab-panel.js REQUIRED

Overrides

@slot nav - Tab buttons (<luna-tab>)
@slot default - Tab panels (<luna-tab-panel>)
@attr placement - Tab position (top, bottom, start, end)
@attr panel - Panel name to activate (on tab)
@attr active - Initially active tab
@attr closable - Show close button on tab
@attr disabled - Disable the tab

Standard Top (with Closable Tabs)

General Statistics Settings Restricted Welcome to the general info panel. Here are some shiny graphs and numbers. Tweak your preferences here.

Bottom Placement

Tab 1 Tab 2 Content for Tab 1 (Indicator on top) Content for Tab 2 (Indicator on top)

Start Placement (Vertical)

Profile Billing User profile details... Manage your subscription.
